12 September 2020

Nanako updated:

  • The crew quarters are now available as an alternate spawnpoint.
  • Maintenance tunnels are now available as a fallback spawnpoint. You can't select it, and will only be sent there if no other options are available.
  • Spawnpoints now take into account the presence of corruption and necromorphs when determining if a spawnpoint is safe.
  • When spawning in cryostorage or dorms, you now start off asleep in a cryo/bed

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • Removed the ability for Roboprinter and Bioprinter circuits to be researched, essentially removing the two from the game.

Terror4000rus updated:

  • Fixed color of some wall frames.
  • You can't stab yourself with tools due crit failure now. Broken lims with artery after laser pickaxe removal is gone.

TheLion1675 updated:

  • CAUTION! This changelog is EXPERIMENTAL! This means these values are NOT permanent and CAN CHANGE in the future without notice.
  • Added the Line Cutter to the exports list with a value of 70 credits.
  • Added the Seeker Rifle to the exports list with a value of 60 credits.
  • Added the Disc Ripper to the exports list with a value of 60 credits.
  • Added the Divet pistol to the exports list with a value of 40 credits.
  • Added the Mining Cutter to the exports list with a value of 40 credits.
  • Addded the Graviton Accelerator to the exports list with a value of 60 credits.
  • Added the Contact Beam to the exports list with a value of 60 credits.
  • New mechanic! End of round now calculates to which side the victory goes. Goes minor/major. Also plays songs depending on the round outcome!
  • Adds regular and compact defibrillators to the medical vendors. This was added because the charges run out REALLY quick.
  • Added Mining Cutter and Plasma Cutter to the research designs. More to come at a later date.
  • Significantly decreased the export worth of Tasers.
  • Significantly decreased the export worth of smelted materials.
  • Moderately increased the Contact Beam's accuracy.
  • Polytrinic Acid rennamed into Triflic Acid.
  • Metal Foam, Iron Foam and Plastic chemistry recipes now no longer require Triflic Acid / Former Polyacid. It now takes Hydrochloric instead.
  • Minorly increased the maximum amount of damage the brain can sustain before becoming a literal Hydro plant.
  • Significantly reduced damage of Triflic Acid / Former Polyacid.
  • Altered recipe for Triflic Acid / Former Polyacid: 1u Lexorin, 1u Potassium Chlorophoride, 1u Hydrochloric Acid makes for 3u Triflic Acid.
  • Moderately reduced the metabolism rate of Tricordrazine. It is still a fix-all, but has the same metabolic stats as Kelotane and Bicaridine.
  • Removed uranium requirement for protolathe Contact Beam construction, and replaced it with Diamond. Don't spam your weapons, use your materials wisely!
  • Increased the weapons technology requirement from 3 to 5 for the Contact Beam.
  • Reworked end of round credits. Now 50 percent more accurate towards how the round goes. LORE FRIENDLY!
  • Significantly reduced the damage received from a toolbox.
  • Minorly increased the damage received from a thrown toolbox.
  • Minorly increased cooldown attacks for toolboxes.
  • Removed the Corgi Crate from Cargo. This is nonsensical. Farm animals is where it's at, boys.
  • Removed a lot of redundant worth list items of items we don't use. Back-end organizing.
  • Removed bluespace tomato as a valid mutation.
  • Fixed inability to make assembly items such as the igniter because it required an item that did not exist.
  • Fixed an uncommon instance of diona nymphs spawning in vending machines. No more leeches!
  • Fixed Botanist uniform displaying incorrect icon. Honk!
  • Fixed Botanist not spawning with their new uniform.

29 August 2020

Nanako updated:

  • Yanking out embedded objects will no longer sever the limb.
  • Fixed material weapons having much less health than intended and breaking too easily.
  • You will no longer attempt to block your own attacks, and a weapon can no longer be used to block attacks from itself.
  • Blocking can now only be done with objects of at least normal size. No more fending off slashers with a pill.
  • Normalised rig and backpack storage. RIGs get a bit more, backpacks get a bit less. A standard rig storage now has the same capacity as a backpack
  • Rebalanced plasma saw and laser pick a bit. Both have slightly lower damage, and higher power consumption, but they now also break more slowly.
  • Lots of new items added to the Tools section of the loadout menu!
  • Added the Rivet Gun, a special new tool for patrons. It can be used for longdistance repairs. amd light but consistent damage output.
  • Tool vendors now stock hacksaws, useful for a variety of things, like making barricades!
  • Added two new crafting recipes related to rivet bolts.
  • Bullets and shrapnel now have a chance to ricochet when hitting solid objects.
  • Necromorphs now recieve a 15% reduction to all incoming damage, while standing on corruption. Should help against spawncamping crew assaults.
  • Increased the strength of movespeed effects from walking on corruption. Necromorph movespeed bonus from 20 to 25%, human speed penalty from 35 to 40%
  • Bladed necromorph basic attacks (Slasher, spitter, twitcher, enhanced slasher) now have minor armor penetration, making them better against crew in tough suits.
  • Slasher max health slightly increased, from 75 to 80. Enhanced slasher adjusted proportionately
  • Slasher biomass reclamation time reduced, from 8 minutes to 7, enhanced from 12 to 11
  • Reduced harvester health regen, from 1 to 0.75, should make it easier to kill with sustained assault.
