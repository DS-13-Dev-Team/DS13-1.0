30 November 2020

TheLion1675 updated:

  • !START OF EXPERIMENTAL BALANCING AND BUGFIXES!
  • The Kellion, Valor and Unitologist ERT types now all have a distinct access datum, locking their ship(s) to just them, including any of their controls. Unless you have an ID Card or have been granted access, you won't be able to hijack, enter or loot it.
  • Kellion team members now spawn with an ERT headset.
  • Kellion team members now spawn with individual rigs.
  • Kellion team members now get HUDs relevant to their position.
  • Increased Kellion's medical capability, which was previously a bit... Underwhelming.
  • Kellion's lockers are now used to store weapons prior to spawning, with one random spawn per round.
  • SecTec added to Kellion.
  • NanoMed Plus added to Kellion.
  • !END OF EXPERIMENTAL BALANCING AND BUGFIXES!
  • Temporarily removed flashbangs, teargas grenades and flashes from the Security vendors. They will be re-added when the intended fixes have been rolled out for Necromorphs getting stunlocked.
  • Removed a few R&D designs that made no sense or weren't for us.
  • Removed the 'Special Ops' crate that could be ordered by Cargo.
  • Laser Pickaxe added to R&D designs. Pick in dem heads! But... Really don't. I'm also a moderator, people. Please don't.
  • Significantly reduced the explosion radius and power of fueltanks. They're not portable mininukes anymore.
  • Reduced folded rollerbeds in size so they can now fit in storage.
  • Changed the item requirements to make a Divet from Silver to Plasteel.
  • Considerably lowered the requirements to make the Price Scanner in R&D.
  • Significantly lowered the requirements of some RIG accessories in R&D. Some of these may not be useful as of yet, but might soon be.
  • Contact Beam Energy significantly increased in cost.
  • Made the Contact Beam moderately more expensive to make.
  • Moderately decreased damage output from advanced circular saw.
  • Made the Contact Beam gun a huge item, whereas previously it was normal.
  • Replaced round end statistics to below the round type victory.
  • Renamed the laser pickaxe to rock saw, according to wiki lore.
  • Pulse High Velocity rounds can now be made by R&D. Their cost and price currently remain something that can be discussed.
  • Experimental fix to the wall of death screen one gets after being knocked unconscious at times.
  • Fixed an issue where the inner cargo warehouse shutter button wouldn't work.
  • Fixed the Colony's railings not being secured at round start.
  • Granted Security, Medical and Research access to the colony.
  • Added a bloodbank to the Aegis Colony Medical outpost.
  • Rejoice for our Science Mains! A fully operational and waiting facility has been added to the Aegis Colony for all your (Xeno)-Archeology desires! Idk, feeling cute, might discover an anomaly later kind of stuff!

Unknown updated:

  • Rebalanced the power of all material melee weapons. Especially the fire axe
  • Certain limbs can no longer be regrown with healing spells. This applies to divider limbs/head, and exploder pustule

20 November 2020

Jeser updated:

  • Increased rules window size.
  • Fixed rules window not loading rules.

Nanako updated:

  • Added the Advanced RIG, a rare, lightweight and tough suit, which is afforded to the chief engineer by default. You might be lucky enough to find one elsewhere
  • Added the Advanced Vital Monitor, a rig module that comes with a built in death alarm to notify emergency services of your untimely demise.
  • Advanced and hacker rig now both have insulated gloves
  • Slightly increased the range of puker's vomit, and reduced its windup time, both by 10%
  • Necromorph acid now metabolises a little faster, thus dealing damage faster. The total damage is unaffected
  • Pulse turrets now only require one power node to create. Formerly two
  • Slightly reduced the max range of the plasma cutter, it may not reach the screen edge when firing diagonally now.
  • Added repair kits, which can be used to perfectly repair other tools, or to repair any kind of object. They can be crafted from easily available materials and items.
  • Added the Tool Expansion recipe, which increases the maximum modification slots on a tool by one, at the cost of a power node.

TheLion1675 updated:

17 October 2020

Nanako updated:

  • Divider puppets can no longer speak common, but will speak on the necrochat instead. Their body will make telltale wailing sounds when they do, so keeping quiet is key to going unnoticed.
  • Added an audible new Signal Ability!
  • Necromorphs now take 10% of incoming damage as Lasting Damage, which does not heal and effectively reduces their maximum health.
  • Slightly buffed the max health of divider, spitter, slasher, leaper and lurker, to partially compensate for the above.
  • Added two new necromorph healing spells - one for signal, and one for marker. Both come with their own drawbacks, but can regrow lost limbs on live necromorphs.

Snypehunter007 updated:

  • Given all bridge staff general belts.
  • Added a divet to the First Lieutenant and Bridge Ensigns belts.

TheLion1675 updated:

  • New skybox background.

10 October 2020

Jeser updated:

  • Fixed charge not dealing damage to lying humans.

Nanako updated:

  • Fixed issues where cyst rotation wouldn't work in certain circumstances. It should now be fully reliable unless the chat entry area is focused
  • Exploder is back and explodier than ever!
  • Object-based shooting abilities now hit a random part of the target instead of always the chest. This currently affects detonator, harvester, and cyst
  • Fixed the rivet gun's fragmentate mode not working.
  • Buffed the rivet fragments' damage, from 1.8 to 2.2
  • Fixed an error message about solnet when spawning as an observer or editing your character.
  • The discord button in the UI is now normally colored.
  • Removed the Lore button from the topright. It contained bay specific stuff which isn't relevant for us.
  • Fixed detonator crate being wrongly named in cargo interface.
  • Majorly increased radiation resistance on the engineering rig. Dedicated radsuits are still slightly better though.
  • Generic pouches now have more reasonable storage capacity
  • Handcuffs now work on people wearing rigs. This feature was originally designed on baycode where rigs were a rare item
  • Fixed sheets in storages getting stuck on screen when merging
  • Material stacks can now fit inside ordinary storage compartments.
  • Rebalanced tool degradation, power use, and failure rates, to generally be more forgiving. Chance of critical failures especially majorly reduced.
  • Construction skill is now factored into tool operations when crafting items.
  • Hauling skill is now factored into worktime and failure rates when digging out rock walls on aegis VII.
  • Brute's slam attack now deals much more damage to dense objects, especially walls.
  • Brute's biobomb attack is now a bit faster to fire and deals more damage.
  • Leaper's tailstrike can no longer hit walls
  • Leaper's biomass cost reduced from 80 to 75
  • Cysts will no longer fire at projectiles or freshly severed limbs
  • Divider puppet now properly counts as a necromorph, and won't be attacked by traps.
  • Fixed some cases where girders were almost impossible to break
  • Throwing items to propel yourself in space now always works
  • Fixed issues of getting stuck when launching out of disposals
  • Tier IV Necromorphs are now spawned with total biomass instead of spending your storage. Total biomass includes the mass currently invested in necromorphs and corruption nodes, so there is no requirement to save up a lump sum.

Terror4000rus updated:

  • Fixed ghost's prefix about the characters real name. So, no more things like '(F)Cody Green/(Unknown(as Cody Green): Cody Green screams!' The prefix will appear only if the character's visual name is 'Unknown' or 'Unknown (as not-real-name)'.

TheLion1675 updated:

  • Fixed an issue where in some instances the end of round music would suddenly switch track. Simplified the code it runs on, and this issue should no longer occur.
  • Fixed a bug where Necromorphs were erroneously counted as surviving crew at the end of the round.
  • Fixed a bug where anyone who would have escaped being marked as marooned on the USG Ishimura.
  • Fixed an uncommon issue where players would be randomly teleported into z-levels with no movement possible at all, if they reached the edge of the level. i.e. Aegis VII.
  • Fixed only suit cyclers being present in Aegis Engineering and Medical Post. One of them is now a cycler, the other a storage unit.
  • Fixed an issue where alive mobs weren't counted as surviving or evacuated crew if the player had ghosted before the calculations occur.
  • Windows for the escape shuttles are now indestructible.
  • Three new end-of-round statistics added as well as variations.
  • All of the ERT shuttles are now marked post-round safezones. This adds onto the escape methods, if you were inclined to give that a shot.
  • Added teleportation if anyone runs out of a shuttle while it is in transit. Should you do this, you will now be warped into space, and your death will be assured. Don't do this.
  • Rework of how escape shuttles work and act. Those who are in the pods now, will be able to walk around on a planetary landing site. If you're really lucky, it won't be infested.
  • Experimental fix for laptops, tablets and PDA's refusing to work after evacuating.
  • Added in appropriate area and landmarks for the Administrative Shuttle. The code for this will likely be included in the same update by KMC.
