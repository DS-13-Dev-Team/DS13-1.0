10 April 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Added the Asteroid Defense System, located on the top deck, above the bridge. It is only accessible via spacewalk along the spine of the ship. The ADS will automatically shoot down incoming asteroids, and it can also be manually controlled. There is currently no need for the manual control, but maybe someday soon it will start breaking down....
  • Majorly revamped meteors. They now come in varying sizes, speeds, and rotation speeds, and they explode when destroyed in flight. Meteors will now always approach on the top deck, but will hit a random deck of the ship when they make contact
  • Meteor events are now enabled and can occur randomly. Meteors will generally come from the front of the ship, and will be shot down by the ADS as long as it's working.
  • Added toggleable blast doors to all the windows on the bridge, and a switch to control them. The outer wall of the bridge can now be turned into a metal shell at a moment's notice.
  • All doors and airlocks now take less damage from explosions.
  • Added more hull plating to the front of the ship on several decks, allowing a few meteor hits to be sustained before hull breaches start to occur.
  • Blast doors are significantly buffed, especially against explosions and meteors.

07 April 2021

Nanako updated:

  • The divider's tongue can now be attacked and cut with melee weapons, to free its victim. This was always intended, it was just bugged until now.
  • Divider components now deal less damage with their basic melee attacks, generally about 25% less.
  • Divider components now deal more damage with their special leap attacks.
  • Divider health and limb health increased.
  • Divider arm swing now has a faster windup animation.
  • Divider Tongue projectile now flies faster.
  • Fixed signals not having sprites

04 April 2021

Nanako updated:

  • Added a new feature for patrons, Necrocustomisation! Allowing you to tweak the random pool for variants and necro outfits. More content will be added to it in time.
  • Instead of being a seperate item, hazmat suits now have their hood built in, it can be toggled with a verb.
  • Hazmat suits now give minor levels of armor. Doesn't compare to a real armored suit, but it's a lot thicker than normal uniforms.
  • Added new sprites for hazmat suits, thanks to Cre77
  • Added several new slasher outfits. thanks to Cre77
