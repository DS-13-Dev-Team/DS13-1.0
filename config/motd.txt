<h1>Welcome to Dead Space 13</h1>

-<i>This server is an unaffiliated fan project based on the Dead Space video game series by Visceral Games and EA. DS13 is a survival-horror roleplay/action game, set aboard the USG Ishimura, prior to the events of Dead Space.
DS13 is running on a heavily modified version of baystation12 code</i>
